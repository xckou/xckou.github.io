COLDPLAY

INTERACTIVE BOOK DESIGN

2016.09 - 2016.10

The "Coldplay" Interactive Book combines several interaction methods such as music, image, lighting with physical book pages, integrate more information transfer modes in the traditional music album, make its have both collection and purchasing value. This project hope to explore a new entity record form and fans consumer market.

WHY A COLDPLAY INTERACTIVE BOOK

Reading is a kind of enjoyment.However, because of the speeding up pace of life pace, and the popularization of digital devices, reading is so easy to become a hastily browsing. In this kind of reading behavior, it is doubtful that how many meaningful content we can get, not to mention the joy of reading we acquire from a tangible book.
